Let's go briefly through what DeepLinks are.

Deep links are a type of link that send users directly to an app instead of a website or a store

* Deep linking does this by specifying a custom URL scheme, that opens your app if it’s already installed.

* The fundamental technology is just a feature of the smartphone’s operating system.

* There by enabling customers to reach desired, without being dragged into unwanted locations.
